*Present day, present time.*

A curated collection of Lain-related websites, resources, and communities. Serial Experiments Lain remains one of the most influential anime series exploring themes of identity, consciousness, and the intersection of technology and humanity.

<!-- more -->

## Visual Resources

- [lain pics](https://github.com/waifod/lain_pics) - A comprehensive GitHub repository of Lain artwork and screenshots
- [Lain Iwakura on Danbooru](https://danbooru.donmai.us/posts?tags=iwakura_lain) - Tagged collection of fan art and official artwork
- [Serial Experiments Lain Wiki](https://lain.fandom.com/) - Comprehensive wiki covering characters, episodes, and lore
- [Lain Aesthetics](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Laincore) - Overview of Laincore aesthetic

## Audio & Music

- [2814 - Birth of a New Day](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F9L4q-0Pi4E) - Classic vaporwave album heavily inspired by Lain's aesthetic
- [Lainchan Radio](https://lainchan.org/radio.html) - 24/7 stream of cyberpunk, vaporwave, and experimental music

## Communities & Discussion

- [Lainchan](https://lainchan.org/) - Imageboard focused on technology, programming, and cyberpunk culture
- [r/Lain](https://www.reddit.com/r/Lain/) - Reddit community for discussion and fan content
- [fauux](https://fauux.neocities.org/) - Cryptic website with Lain-inspired aesthetics and hidden content
